About Us

Based in Washington D.C.

The D*Minus Company was founded in 2017 with the intention to promote adrenaline sports. It has since become primarily a skateboarding brand and was taken in a direction we thought not possible in such a short time with the help of skaters themselves, primarily Giavante Bolar. We're continuing to grow and expand and we welcome you along our journey with us.
